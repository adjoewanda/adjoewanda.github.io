Bio

New York City based Web Developer and Designer with a specialization in Front End Development and Visual Design. Anthony studied in New York City and graduated from City University of New York (CUNY), Baruch College, with a Bachelor's Degree in Advertising. He concentrated on his studies in Graphic Design through Baruch Digital Design Program. He also continued his design studies further at Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) and NYU School of Professional Studies. With the ever-increasing interaction between programming and real-world design projects, he enrolled in a one year Software Development Program at City University of New York (CUNY), QCC. Originally from Indonesia and previously from Singapore, where he briefly spent his childhood, he moved abroad when he was a kid and lived with his relatives in US.
